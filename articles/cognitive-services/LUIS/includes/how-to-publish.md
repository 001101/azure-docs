In order to receive a LUIS prediction in a chat bot or other client application, you need to publish the app to the endpoint. 

1. Select **Publish** in the top right navigation.

    ![LUIS publish to endpoint button in top right menu](./media/publish-button.png)

1. Select the **Production** slot and the **Publish** button.

    <!-- TBD: add image -->

1. Publishing is complete when you see the green status bar at the top of the website confirming success.

    ![LUIS publish to endpoint](./media/publish-to-endpoint-success.png)

1. Select the **endpoints** link in the green status bar to go to the **Azure Resources** page. The endpoint URLs are listed at the bottom.
