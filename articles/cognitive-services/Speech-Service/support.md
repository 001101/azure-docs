 - [C# service library-related questions](https://github.com/Azure-Samples/Cognitive-Speech-STT-ServiceLibrary/issues)

 - [Objective-C library on iOS](https://github.com/Azure-Samples/Cognitive-Speech-STT-iOS/issues)
